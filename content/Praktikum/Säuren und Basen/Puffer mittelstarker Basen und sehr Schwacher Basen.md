$K_A = \dfrac{[H_3O^+] * [B]}{[A]}$
                                                    
Vereinfacht gilt                                                  
                                                    
$[A] = [A]_0 - [H_3O^+]$                                                  
                                                    
$[B] = [B]_0 + [H_3O^+]$                                                  
                                                  
Es folgt:                                                  
                                                  
  $[H_3O^+]^2 + [H_3O^+] * (K_A+[B]_0) - K_A * [A]_0 = 0$                                                  
                                                    
Mit der pq Formel:                                                  
                                                  
> [!important] $[H_3O^+] = - \dfrac{K_A + [B]_0}{2} + \sqrt{\dfrac{K_A + [B]_0}{2}^2 + K_A * [A]_0}$
